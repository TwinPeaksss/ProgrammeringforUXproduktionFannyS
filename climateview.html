ClimateView

This is a student project where I together with four class students made a research study for the company ClimateView where we focused on the first two phases in the design process of double diamond. The purpose of the study was for us to gain knowledge and understanding of how to use different UX-research methods, as well as giving our client valuable insights about the target group.
